Pettie COMMUNITY

Pettie community is a platform for those who can no longer take care of their pets, and to those who needs a pet. Owners will get face to face interactions with each other and have a smooth transaction between cat owners. It’s will be easy for people who wants a pet, where all the vaccines and materials are already taken cared of. It will also be convenience for people who need to travel or move locations and any other reason that they can’t take care of their animals anymore. So they do not have to sent them to pet shelters.
